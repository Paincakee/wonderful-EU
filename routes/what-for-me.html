What Has the EU Done for Me (and My Daily Life)?

As a young citizen of the EU, the impact of EU policies and initiatives can be felt in various aspects of daily life:

Travel and Mobility: The EU's Schengen Area allows for seamless travel across borders without passport controls, making it easier to explore different countries and cultures.

Education and Opportunities: Programs like Erasmus+ offer scholarships and exchange opportunities for studying abroad, broadening horizons and fostering personal growth.

Consumer Protection: EU regulations ensure the safety and quality of products and services, providing peace of mind when making purchases or transactions.

Digital Rights: The EU's General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) safeguards personal data and privacy rights, empowering individuals to control their online presence and information.

Environmental Awareness: EU initiatives promote sustainability and environmental conservation, encouraging eco-friendly practices and raising awareness about climate change and biodiversity loss.
